What is tw33tyPy?

tweetyPy is a project by Xristos Katsaros, dedicated to analyzing trending topics on Twitter using Natural Language Processing and other twitter statistics. This version is focused on a collection of 480,000+ tweets between December 1st, 2019 and February 4th, 2020.
